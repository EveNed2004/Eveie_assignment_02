An article about seating.

~ Most often the band is set up in a semicircle shape, if you have ever observed a band preform you may have already noticed this.

~ The band can be broken up into 4 different parts essentially it comes down too, Brass, Woodwind, String, and Percussion. Each of these sections provide a different type of sound and because some instruments do not produce the same amount of sound as others the placement for these matters on order for them to be heard properly.

~ For example the Flute and Clarinet instruments are considered woodwind, they often carry a harmony or a melody, but compared to trumpet or a tuba the sound is much softer in comparison so if the trombone or tuba or saxophone were placed in front then the flutes sound would be cancled out because the brass instruments which produce a much louder sound would overpower the softer elements of the woodwind sound.
